Every physical entity has a Position; within the scope of ITS (surface transport), this location should be on the surface of the earth. <br/>Physical entities may be able to detect their location via a variety of technologies; many of these will report the location using a GeoLocation, which is known as the geoPosition of the PhysicalEntity. <br/><br/>
