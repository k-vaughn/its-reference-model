ambientAirTemperature - reports the 'Temperature' of the ambient air around the vehicle; the value of location shall always be zero (0) for this type.<br/><br/>ambientAirPressure - reports the 'Pressure' of the ambient air around the vehicle; the value of location shall always be zero for this type.<br/><br/>tirePressure - reports the 'Pressure' of the specified tire of the vehicle. Location shall be 1 for left front, 2 for right front, 3 for left rear, and four for right rear.  (NOTE, this probably has to be expanded to support more tires)<br/><br/>etc.<br/>
