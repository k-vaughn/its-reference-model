The preferred standard units and orderOfMagnitude for the reading are defined by the "value" attribute of this mid-level entity. All mid-level entities should have a "value" attribute. In this case, it would specify the standard SI unit of Pascals and an order of magnitude of -3 to indicate milliPascals. The mid-level entity may have other attributes that provide alternate representations of the data (e.g., enumerations, such as "low", "medium", "high") or other details (e.g., the type of precipitation).<br/><br/>Implementations can use other units, but they should be translatable to the standard units with translation errors causing corresponding increases to the accuracy range.<br/>
